Privacy Policy

Effective date: Feb 25, 2026

STRYD collects basic account and workout information to provide core app features like syncing, matchups, and goal tracking.

Information we collect

  • Account info: name, email, and profile details you provide.
  • Workout data: activity type, duration, points, and timestamps.
  • Optional media: profile image if you upload one.

How we use data

  • Provide app functionality and sync across devices.
  • Display matchups, goals, and progress.
  • Improve app reliability and support.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Data may be stored with service providers (e.g. Firebase) to enable app functionality.

Data retention

Data is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.
